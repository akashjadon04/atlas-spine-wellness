Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common patient inquiries.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we are in-network with Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, UnitedHealthcare, and Medicare. For other providers, we offer competitive self-pay rates and can provide superbills.

Do I need a referral?

In the state of Texas, chiropractors are primary care providers, meaning you generally do NOT need a referral to book an appointment with us.

How long is the first visit?

New patient consultations typically last 45-60 minutes. This includes your history, examination, and digital imaging if necessary.

What is your cancellation policy?

We respectfully request 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may be subject to a $50 fee.
