Our Story

From Passion to Practice

Founded in the heart of Austin, Atlas Spine & Wellness began with a singular mission: to bridge the gap between traditional chiropractic adjustments and modern rehabilitative technology.

We recognized that US healthcare often treats symptoms, not causes. Our clinic was built to challenge that status quo. By integrating bio-structural data with compassionate, hands-on care, we have successfully treated over 5,000 patients ranging from professional athletes to retirees.
